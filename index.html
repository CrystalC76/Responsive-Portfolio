About Me

Hello! My name is Crystal R. Cumberledge, and I am an aspiring web developer!
I have been a hard worker since the day I turned 15! I have always been in the customer service field. My previous jobs include: Winn-Dixie, McDonald's, Pep Boys, Clicks Billiards, The Bank Vault, Trick Shots Billiards, and lastly Micrologix Information Systems. I have bagged groceries, been a team leader, sold auto-parts, ran a commercial sales department, cocktail server, bartender, bar manager, and executive assistant. I have always excelled at any position I have held! Through these jobs I developed amazing skills like the ability to multitask, prioritze, organize, lead, problem solve, adapt, and manage stress. I also learned how to deliver excellent customer service by developing relationships, understanding their needs, and use my innate communication and interpersonal skills. I thrive on a sense of accomplishment, and love to be challenged!

Even though I was born in Pennsylvania, I have been in central Florida since I was 4 months old. So, depending on who you ask, I am pretty much a Florida native! What I love about Florida is the proximity to the ocean, the BEAUTIFUL weather (3 months out of the year- summer can suck it!- ha ha), and the theme parks! I am a roller coaster junkie!

I chose this field for several reasons. I wanted to be able to make more money than what I had been for years. Also, I am good at math, I like creating things, and I have always been a good problem solver. What I hope to accomplish by following this path, is a career that I enjoy, I can grow in, feel challenged in, and have the potential to increase my income. Here's to a brighter future!
